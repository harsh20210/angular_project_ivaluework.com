Soft skills

Soft skills are the personal attributes and interpersonal skills that enable us to interact effectively and harmoniously with other people. They are essential for success in all areas of our lives, from our personal relationships to our professional careers.

Some key soft skills include:

  • Communication
  • Teamwork
  • Critical thinking
  • Creativity
  • Adaptability
  • Leadership
  • Emotional intelligence
  • Time management
  • Stress management
  • Conflict resolution
  • Negotiation

Soft skills are becoming increasingly important in the workplace, as employers are looking for employees who can not only perform their technical duties, but also work effectively with others and contribute to a positive work environment.
